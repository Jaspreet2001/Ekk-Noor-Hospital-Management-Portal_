ESTABLISHED IN SERVICE

Our Story in Alamgir

Rooted in the spirit of 'Neki' (goodness), Ek Noor Hospital Alamgir stands as a beacon of hope, delivering professional medical care to those who need it most.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to the community of Alamgir and surrounding areas, ensuring no one is left behind due to financial hardship.

✨

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in charitable healthcare, where technology meets compassion, creating a healthier future for every family in our region.

Our Journey

A Legacy of Care

The Foundation

Started as a small clinic in Alamgir with a vision to serve the underprivileged, driven by local volunteers and dedicated doctors.

Growth & Expansion

Recognizing the growing needs, we expanded into a multi-specialty hospital with advanced surgical units and diagnostic labs.

Modern Era

Today, we integrate digital health records and modern equipment while keeping our charitable heart at the center of everything.
